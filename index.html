About A filmmaker in motion

I’m Ryder Thomas, a 16-year-old cinematographer, filmmaker, and photographer. My world revolves around the way light tells a story — from early golden hours to neon midnight frames. I craft visuals that feel intimate and emotionally rich, refining each shot through color grading and careful editing in After Effects, Premiere Pro, and DaVinci Resolve. Every project is a chance to sculpt color, pace, and emotion into something that lingers.
