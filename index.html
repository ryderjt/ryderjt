Ryder Thomas

Cinematographer · Filmmaker · Photographer

I’m a 16-year-old cinematographer who chases light, motion, and emotion. I blend color, texture, and rhythm to craft visuals that feel intimate and alive — from dreamy film stills to dynamic edits that pull you in.

About

Stories have been my favorite escape for as long as I can remember. I started editing as a kid, experimenting in After Effects, Premiere Pro, and DaVinci Resolve until layers of color, motion, and sound began to click. Now I’m obsessed with building cinematic moments — the kind that linger, feel nostalgic, and glow like you remember them.

I love taking raw footage and sculpting it into something immersive. Whether it’s color grading to create a specific mood, designing motion that feels effortless, or framing shots that feel like memories, my goal is to make every project personal and alive.
