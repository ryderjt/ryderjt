About

I’m Ryder Thomas, a 16-year-old cinematographer crafting intimate narratives through lens, light, and motion. I pull emotion out of color, frame rhythm with sound, and cut films with a painter’s patience.

I live for the in-between moments: the flicker before a smile, the pulse of a bassline, the hush of a scene just before it erupts. Every project is a chance to experiment, to stretch a feeling into a sequence, and to make the ordinary feel cinematic.
