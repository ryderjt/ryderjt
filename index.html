About

I’m Ryder Thomas, a 16-year-old cinematographer, filmmaker, and photographer driven by the way color and composition can translate emotion. My work centers around grounded visuals that feel alive—immersive moments captured in natural light, subtle movement, and authentic pacing. I bring years of editing experience in After Effects, Premiere Pro, and DaVinci Resolve, crafting narrative tone through color grading, timing, and sound.
