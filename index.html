About
Direct and minimal

I am Ryder Thomas, a 16-year-old self-taught visual storyteller. I work across cinematography, photography, and design, covering planning, shooting, editing, and color to keep one visual language.

My projects include short films, documentary-style pieces, social content, and still photography. I shoot portraits, editorial stories, brand visuals, and lookbooks; I scout locations, style, and deliver full Lightroom edit passes.

Film and motion work includes concept planning, shot design, camera and lighting direction, editing, sound, cinematic color, and motion graphics/VFX with clear review rounds and fast turnarounds. Tools: DaVinci Resolve, After Effects, Lightroom, Premiere Pro. Portfolio date: 2026.
